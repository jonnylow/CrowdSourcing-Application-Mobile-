WHAT IS CAREGUIDE
CareGuide is a social crowdsourcing mobile application that makes volunteering as easy as scan, apply and volunteer! CareGuide volunteers will be able to sign up for transportation voluntary activities posted by Centre For Seniors using the mobile application. Volunteers will be accompanying the elderly from Centre For Seniors to and through their medical appointment.
CENTRE FOR SENIORS
Centre For Seniors is a voluntary welfare organisation that is committed to promote the total well-being of older persons in Singapore. They currently have 26 daycare centre as well as 20 sheltered homes in Singapore.
